Visting Volunteers

Visiting Volunteers make regular visits to older adults who are physically and emotionally isolated. Program staff and volunteers help reconnect these older adults to people and resources in their community, increase their independence, and create friendships.

Volunteers are matched with participants according to interests, personality and geography.Greatest Grandkids asks that its Visiting Volunteers spend six hours per month with the person they are supporting, for a minimum period of 6 months. Visiting Volunteers are supervised by a Volunteer Liasiohn. If interested, please contact Jane or Harriet.If you are interested in any of our volunteering opportunities, please register your interest using the form here.

Benefits Duties and Responsibilities Qualifications
1. Gain gratification in knowing that the isolation of an older adult has been relieved 1. Visit your older friend for 6 hours a month for the time period of 6 months 1. Desire to enhance the well-being of older adults
2. Develop new friendships and share new experiences 2. Act as liaison between the person you spend time with and Greatest Grandkids by reporting any concerns about health and well-being to the Volunteer Liaisosn 2. Sensitivity to the needs of lonely and isolated older adults facing difficult life experiences
3. Gain experience in the field of aging 3. Follow the policies of the organization 3. Effective communication skills
4. Develop and enhance skills and abilities 4. Respect the confidences of the recipients of our services and the Greatest Grandkids organization 4. Ability to make commitments and follow through
5. Attend Greatest Grandkids celebrations, activities, and workshops 5. Demonstrate commitment, reliability, and responsibility 5. Flexibility and an open mind to diversity
6. Attend on-going training and workshops 6. Provide feedback, suggestions, and recommendations to appropriate staff concerning the effectiveness of Greatest Grandkids programs
7. Obtain support and technical assistance 7. Inform Greatest Grandkids staff or Volunteer Liaisons if you are unable to continue volunteer duties
