Why Volunteer

Volunteering provides an opportunity to become involved in your community, meet other people and make a positive contribution, it is a means of enhancing specific skills and talents, personal development and self-esteem. The experience gained doing volunteer work offers an advantage if you feel that you wish to pursue a paid career.Voluteers will gain gratification in knowing that the isolation of an older adult has been relieved. Develop new friendships and share new experiences while gaining experience in the field of aging. It also helps to develop and enhance skills and abilities. Volunteers also have oppotunities to attend Greatest Grandkids celebrations, activities, and workshops, on-going training and workshops and obtain support and technical assistance if needed.

The personal benefits to you as a volunteer are that you get to participate and be actively involved in the community. It will help you develop and share personal skills, life with the community. You have oppotunity to evelop and share work skills and knowledge and gain valuable career experience. Helping someone else will make you feel needed by society. Through trying somthing different, you will be able to build self eseteem and confidence. Finally, meeting different people will develop your social skills and expand your network.
