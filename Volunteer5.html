Pen or Phone Pals

If you would like to volunteer but are unable to make the time commitment, or live outside of our catchment area, you can volunteer to became a pen or phone pal for an elderly person. Phone Pals place regular calls to an older person to help alleviate lonilness and help that person feel connected and part of the broader community. Pen pals write letters and greeting cards to help alleviate loneliness and help those we serve feel connected and part of the broader community.

Minimal qulifications for phone pals: Pleasant phone voice and customer service and good oral communication skills are essential to this. You have to be an excellent listener with an empathetic and non-judgmental attitude. Have a good understanding and sensitivity to the needs, attitudes, and behavior of older adults. Always stay reliable and self-motivated and interacts well with older population. Minimum age of 18 years is required and for each of these roles, we ask that you commit to 2-3 contacts per month per phone or pen pal.

Minimal qulifications for pen pals:You should have a strong reading and writing skills and a good penmanship. We will expect you to enjoy correspondence writing and comprehend oral and written language (English and/or Spanish preferred). Also be reliable and self-motivated and interact well with older population. Most importantly, you have to be a good reader or listener.
